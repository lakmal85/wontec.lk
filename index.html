Sales Department

The Sales Department is responsible for increasing sales, maintaining customer relationships, and supporting the service process.

Engineering Department

The Engineering Department is responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining products and systems, ensuring technical solutions meet quality and performance standards.

Finance Department

The Finance Department manages the organization’s financial activities, including budgeting, forecasting, financial reporting, and ensuring compliance with financial regulations.

After Sales Department

The After Sales Department ensures customer satisfaction post-purchase by providing support, handling inquiries, managing warranties, and resolving issues related to products and services.

IT Department

The IT Department is responsible for managing and maintaining the organization’s technology infrastructure, ensuring system security, providing technical support, and optimizing digital resources.
